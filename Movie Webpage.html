What Are Movies?

Movies are stories protrayed through pieces of film that are used to entertain people for an extended period of time. Usually movies can be seen in movie theaters, but recently with advancements in technology, there are new services that allow for poeple to view movies at home or on a portable device. There are also many differnent types of movies. There are the live action ones where the stories take place in real life, and there are also animated movies. This is where the stories take place in a world created through computer software. These are just some among many others.
