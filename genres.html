Genres

What Are Genres?

Genres are basically categories used to be able to distinguish things from one another. They are not only used in film but in other things as well like literature. Here are some movie genres that are common today:

  • Action
  • Drama
  • Romance
  • Comedy
  • Horror
  • Science Fiction
  • Fantasy
